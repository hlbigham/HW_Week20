About Me

I knew what I wanted to do with my life when I was fourteen trying to explain trigonometry to my mother. I have this unwavering need to help people understand things. Writing is about communicating, and communicating is about understanding. I write to help users understand a product, from the microcopy on buttons and error messages to the clear and exact language in a technical manual on a submarine.

Understanding is my approach to problem solving: I want to know your users and your product to help you make something that’s straightforward and effortless. Currently, I'm applying this approach as a Technical and UI Writer at Workday.
